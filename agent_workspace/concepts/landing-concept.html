How it works

Three steps to sharper practice.

Use Practapal after any session to understand what happened, and walk away with a clear plan.

1

Upload your score

Add the sheet music for the piece you are working on. MusicXML or image — both work.

2

Record your take

Upload audio or video from your practice session. Any quality, any device.

3

Get precise feedback

See exactly which measure, which beat, and what to do about it — tied directly to your score.

Core Feature

Feedback tied to your score, not a chat bubble.

Practapal reads your recording and your sheet music together. Every flag points to a specific measure. Every flag has a Loop button so you can hear exactly what went wrong, right from the analysis view.

  • Measure-level pitch, timing, and dynamics analysis
  • One-click Loop playback on every flagged section
  • Sheet music stays visible on desktop — always
  • AI coach gives specific, actionable advice
  • Track improvement across takes over time

What Practapal helps with

Built around how musicians actually practice.

Measure-level precision

No vague "watch your timing." You get the exact beat, the exact measure, with the number of milliseconds you were off.

Loop any flagged section

One click to hear the exact 4-beat window where the issue happened. Listen as many times as you need.

AI coach that knows your music

Ask follow-up questions about your take. The coach already has your analysis data — it never asks what notes you played.

Progress across takes

Upload a second take on the same piece and see what improved, what regressed, and what stayed the same.

Score always in view

The sheet music stays on screen while you review feedback. No tab-switching, no losing your place.

Built-in tuner & metronome

Real autocorrelation pitch detection and tap-tempo metronome, available any time from the sidebar.
